Creamy Cottage Cheese Scrambled Eggs

Cottage cheese scrambled eggs for breakfast are a nice change from regular scrambled eggs. This egg recipe comes out creamy and soft. Perfect with a slice of tomato and turkey bacon for a delicious and fast low-carb breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 tablespoon butter
  • Eggs: 4 large eggs, beaten
  • Soy sauce: ¼ cup cottage cheese
  • Chives: 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives, or to taste (Optional)
  • Salt & Black Pepper: Ground black pepper & salt to taste

Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour beaten eggs into the skillet; let cook undisturbed until the bottom of the eggs begins to firm, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Stir cottage cheese and chives into eggs and season with black pepper.
  4. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, 3 to 4 minutes more.
